Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Move It is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Chrome extension.

What We Do NOT Collect

Move It does not collect, store, or process any personal data such as:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Browsing history or visited websites
  • Keyboard inputs or screenshots
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or personal information

Move It operates entirely on your local machine. The extension functions independently without requiring any account creation or personal information.

Analytics

Move It uses Google Analytics to understand how the extension is used. This helps us improve the extension and understand which features are most valuable to users.

Google Analytics collects the following types of anonymous usage information:

  • How often the extension is used
  • When movement reminders are triggered
  • General usage patterns and frequency
  • Browser and device type information

This data is completely anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not track what websites you visit or any content you interact with.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is subject to Google’s own privacy policies. For more information about how Google processes data, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to delete or request. However, you can always:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Disable analytics tracking through your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics as described above

Extension Permissions

Move It requests certain permissions to function properly. These permissions are used solely for the extension’s core functionality:

  • Alarms: To set and manage movement break reminders at your specified intervals
  • Notifications: To display movement exercises when your reminder is triggered
  • Storage: To save your extension settings locally on your device

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your settings or preferences to any external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Your continued use of the extension constitutes your acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please reach out through moveit.work.
