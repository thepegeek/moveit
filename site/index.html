Simple by design

Three steps. That’s it.

No apps to open. No routines to remember. Move It works quietly in the background until it’s time to move.

1

Set your interval

Pick how often you want a reminder — anywhere from 5 to 55 minutes. Set it once and forget it.

2

Get your exercise

A new browser tab opens with a simple, desk-friendly activity. No equipment. No gym clothes required.

3

Mark it done

Hit Done and get back to work. Your streak updates automatically. Repeat until you’re unstoppable.

Why people love it

Built for people who sit too much

Whether you work in deep focus or bounce between tabs, Move It fits your rhythm.

🧠

ADHD & Focus Friendly

Works perfectly with the Pomodoro Technique. Take your movement break at the end of every focus block and come back sharper than you left.

🕒

Work Hours Mode

Only want reminders between 9 and 5? Set your working hours and days and Move It stays quiet outside them. No alerts on weekends.

💪

Desk-Friendly Moves

Every activity is designed for the office or home desk. Stretches, squats, breathing exercises — things you can actually do in 60 seconds.

🔀

160+ Activities

A huge variety of exercises so you never see the same one twice in a row. Shuffle for a different one if today’s suggestion isn’t your thing.

✅

Daily Progress

See how many activities you’ve completed today. Small wins add up to a big habit — and Move It tracks every one of them.

🔒

Private by Default

No account. No sign-up. Your settings live in your browser. We don’t track what you do, only basic usage events so we can improve the extension.
